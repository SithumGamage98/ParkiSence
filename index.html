AI based Parkinson Disease Detection and Treatment Recommendation System

Literature Survey




Welcome to our research website

The Parkinson’s disease is a prominent neurodegenerative disorder which has been causing a significant effect on the health of global populations . It has been observed that, with the effect of the disease, an increasing number of people have been diagnosed with disability conditions over these many years. The disease has been making major effects on the nervous system of the subjective individuals along with their body movements which are controlled by those nerves . As determined, the symptoms of the disease appear slowly and then grow up gradually, subsequently leading to some clearly observable features on the internal and external parts of the human body . Under these conditions, the development of a specialized and more efficient Artificial Intelligence system with pre trained Machine Learning and Deep Learning models for the convenient diagnosis of the Parkinson’s disease and proposing of necessary treatment actions on it has been identified as a major necessity . Most exclusively, the intelligent system which is concerned in here has been designed for supporting the workflow of a Parkinson’s treatment unit belonging to a healthcare institution.

When dealing with the Parkinson patients who are seeking the treatment support, the medical practitioners have often come up with many issues and problems . In order to provide the patients with most suitable treatments for their respective Parkinson conditions, these medical practitioners are in need of precisely identifying and clarifying the disease without falling for wrongful assumptions . This would eventually decide the effectiveness of the treatments provided on the patients. As determined by the experts in the medical field, the early detection of the Parkinson’s disease is possible from the signs shown by the body of an individual under some of the special circumstances . The pressure exerted by the fingers when performing typing and pressing actions is a perfect example for such instance . Also, this early clarification of the disease symptoms might become a greater advantage for the medical practitioners in many different ways. Allowing the medical practitioners to take immediate precautionary actions against the disease condition while avoiding its further development would be the major one of those benefits.

Research Gap




1. Early symptomatic detection of the Parkinson’s disease from the pressure exerted by fingers when performing typing actions

This specific component has been designed to identifiy the Parkinson disease from the symptoms shown at its early development stage. The assumed patients of the Parkinson’s disease will be provided with some pressure sensor attached IoT device and then they would be allowed to create some pressure with their palm and the fingers put on the special pressure points of that device. Mostly that will be like performing some typing actions with the fingers and caprturing their subsequent intensities. Therefater, those readings will be fed in to the system and in case if there is any early sign of the Parkinson’s disease, the AI system will notify it and present some appropriate treatment recommendations.

2. Determining of the progress of the Parkinson’s disease over the time from the characteristics shown by the images of hand drawn spiral shapes

It is very important to decide the severity stage of the disease when a person with Parkinson conditions has been forwarded to get treatments from a medical practitioner. Also, when it comes to a patient who has already taken treatments, it has to be discovered whether the previous treatments have been worked on the patient and the patient has been relieved from the disease or else the symptoms have been developed gradually. Here, in this proposed system, the patients will be given with the opportunity to draw on some sketched spiral shapes on the papers. These drawings will be then scanned and fed to the system to determine the disease stage of the particular patient.

3. Clarification of the effects caused by Parkinson disease on the human body, through the MRI scanned images of human brain

The Parkinson’s disease would create a significant impact on certain muscular movements of the human body. So that, the determining of the symptoms of Parkinson’s disease on the human brain becomes a very useful approach to calibrate this effect caused by the Parkinson up to a certain extent. Here, an image generated by conducting a MRI scan on the patient’s brain is used as the source for disease detection. This image is uploaded to the system and with its encompassed capabilities, the system will be identifying the Parkinson affected regions of the brain. Followed by that, the system will be forwarding certain treatments which might be effective on the particular patient to recover from the disease.

4. Identifying the impact of Parkinson’s disease on the speaking activities of individuals by tracking their vocal frequencies

The normal speaking style of the individuals would get directly impacted from the development of Parkinson symptoms. When it comes to the functionalities offered by this specific system, the identification of the Parkinson through the vocal frequency data and forwarding those patients on some preventive measures could be considered as very important ones. Here at first, the vocal frequency data of the Parkinson patient will be captured while he or she is speaking in a noraml setting. These data will be then converted and fed to the system, thus declaring any symptomatic signs of the Parkinson on the patient. Also, some treatment recommendations would come all along the way from the system, in case if any trace of the disease is found in the vocal frequency data.

Research Problem & Solution




Proposed Problem

The intelligent system for the detection of Parkinson’s disease and recommendation of treatments on it will be developed using the Machine Learning aspects. The expertise on the field of health and medicine specialized on Neurology will be utilized to gather the knowledge needed in formalizing these detection and prediction criteria of Parkinson disease. The Machine Learning models will be trained with those acquired information to carry out the detection proceedings of the disease with higher accuracy and enhanced level of usability.

Proposed Solution

The necessity of a well-organized Artificial Intelligence based system for achieving the Parkinson’s disease diagnostics and as well as facilitating the treatment recommendations on the disease is highlighted from the major objectives of this research. As determined earlier, in here, four specific sub systems have been incorporated with each other in a well-designed and perfectly built mobile application to cater for these most crucial requirements and goals of the research. Especially, this each individual research component has been designed to address some unique challenges which are faced by the Parkinson patients and as well as the medical practitioners who have been conducting treatments on those patients in a healthcare setting.
